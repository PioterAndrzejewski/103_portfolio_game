This is an application for an architectural office that calculates the initial valuation of a design study.

The project is valued on the basis of the valuation guidelines - a document promoted by the Association of Polish Architects.

Project consists of front-end app that is communicating with back-end app, which is responsible for calculating the quote and make decision which image should be displayed.

Technologies: React, Context API, Sass Modules, Chakra UI, Figma, Node, and Express
